The Challenge

Institutional clients, particularly ETF providers, operate under strict regulatory frameworks. They require guarantees that retail staking solutions simply don't offer. Slashing protection, insurance wrappers, and complete auditability of the validator provisioning process were non-negotiable requirements for entry. The market lacked a solution that combined the yield of native staking with the security of cold storage custody.

The Solution

We architected a validator provisioning system that abstracted the complexity of node management while providing granular control over rewards and reporting. By leveraging secure enclaves (AWS Nitro), we ensured that validator keys were generated and signed without ever being exposed to human operators or internet-connected memory.

The interface was designed for operations teams, providing a "one-click" staking experience that handled the complex state transitions of the Beacon chain in the background. We introduced a multi-sig governance layer for protocol upgrades, ensuring no single point of failure.

Key Insight

"Security isn't just about key management; it's about operational resilience. We built the system to survive partial cloud outages without risking liveness penalties, using geographically distributed fallback nodes."
